Full description:
This application adds York Region YRT Viva buses information to MonTransit.

This app contains the buses schedule (offline & real-time) and the latest news from www.yrt.ca, YouTube and @YRTViva on Twitter.

YRT Viva serves the York Region in Ontario, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by York Region Transit and Viva.
http://www.yrt.ca/en/aboutus/developer.asp

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-york-region-yrt-viva-bus-android

This app is not related with York Region Transit and Viva.

Permissions:
- Other: required for real-time schedule & service alerts and to read news from www.yrt.ca, YouTube and Twitter.
